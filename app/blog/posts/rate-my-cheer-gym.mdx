## Project Overview

**Rate My Cheer Gym** connects the cheerleading community with a comprehensive directory of gyms across the United States. Users can explore gyms, leave ratings, and write reviews to share their experiences‚Äîfostering transparency and informed choices in the cheer industry.

## My Role

I handled the full lifecycle from architecture to deployment.

- **Frontend:** Next.js for fast, SEO-optimized, and dynamic UI.
- **Backend:** Supabase for PostgreSQL database, APIs, and authentication.
- **UI:** Tailwind CSS for responsive, accessible layouts.
- **Infra:** Deployed on Cloudflare with CDN caching for global performance.
- **Collaboration:** Designed user flows for rating, reviewing, and browsing gyms with community feedback in mind.
