## Project Overview

**Get Optimise** helps teams measure what matters and act fast. Users run audits, compare baselines, and follow targeted recommendations to lift real-world performance and SEO.

## My Role

I owned the full lifecycle‚Äîarchitecture, development, deployment, and polish.

- **Frontend:** Next.js with dynamic data views and chart components.
- **Backend:** Node.js + Express, PostgreSQL for durable storage.
- **Infra:** VPS deploy via **Coolify** with **Nginx** reverse proxy, Dockerized services.
- **Collaboration:** Translated product goals into UI flows, and iterated quickly on feedback.

## What I Optimised

- Reduced initial TTFB via Nginx caching and tuned upstream timeouts.
- Cut bundle size with route-level code-splitting and image optimization.
- Shortened audit runs using queued workers and concurrency caps.
